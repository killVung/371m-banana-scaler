Banana Scaler

Wanna "Banana for Scale"? We have an option for you!

View on GitHub

Draw on pictures with bananas, for scaling purpose.

A simple app that helps user to draw banana on any images they like, to testify what can humans do with their mobile devices, as well as jumping on the bandwagon of Banana for Scale as quickly as possible.

Features

  • Draw banana on any pictures captured from a camera
  • Draw banana on any pictures accessed from a gallery
  • Easy Intersection: TAP to draw, HOLD to Save / Delete pictures
